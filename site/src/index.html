Rin

A Discord bot focused on obtaining data from third-party services

collections

A Wide Collection of Services Supported

Rin supports a wide variety of services, allowing to obtain data from these services just from within Discord (For example, you can request some art from DeviantArt, or search up whether a Minecraft server is online or not). Such services include Hypixel, Instagram, and many more. More will be coming in the near future, including Twitch.

speed

Optimized for Performance

Rin is designed with performance in mind. Under the hood, Rin uses Uvloop, which is 2 times faster than Node.js. And all code is executed asynchronously, thus speeding up performance.

Completely Free and Open Source

Rin's source code remains completely free and open source, and it is licensed under Apache-2.0. This allows for a level of transparency, and thus allowing anyone to contribute to the bot if they wish so. In short, it's not dangerous to use.
